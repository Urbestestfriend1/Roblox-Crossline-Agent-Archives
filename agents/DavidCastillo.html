AGENT DOSSIER – DAVID CASTILLO

Codename: AXIOM

Division: ALTA Recon

Age: 29

Background

David Castillo grew up in a border-town community shaped by cartel violence and paramilitary pressure. His early volunteer rescue work exposed him to the realities of asymmetric warfare and the devastating impact of misinformation. Recruited into ALTA at 24, Castillo became one of the youngest field strategists to successfully complete the “Blind Corridor” assessment.

Psych Profile

Highly analytical. Displays a pattern of emotional detachment during operations. A ███████████ incident at age 17 remains his only noted psychological trigger.

Field Capabilities
